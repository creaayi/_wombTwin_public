wombTwin — palette of directions

01 — What determines the tiles
A
Testimony-driven proportions
Tile width = time spent on each emotional state in the testimony. Euphoria is short. Grief is long. The proportions come from a real human account, not invention.
source: testimony
B
Aikāne timestamp proportions
Real timecodes from your analysis (4:28, 11:17, 12:16, 12:50...) become the grid. The reference film's own rhythm becomes the scaffolding.
source: aikāne
C
You sculpt them by feel
Tiles are draggable. You resize until the rhythm feels true. What you produce becomes the actual brief. The tool is a composition instrument, not a presentation.
source: your hand
D
Music-locked grid
The grid snaps to bars and phrases. The composer sets the tempo and the tiles align to it. The film's architecture becomes literally musical from the start.
source: composer
E
Symptom-weighted density
Tile height varies per register. Grief gets more vertical space because it was always larger than it appeared. The twin track starts thin and grows. Proportion as diagnosis.
source: the wound
F
Hybrid — testimony × your hand
Start from testimony proportions. Then you adjust. The real account is the skeleton; your feelingmagining is the flesh. Two authors, one score.
source: both
